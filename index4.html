About Me

Hello reader, my name is Christopher Herrmann, and this is a little about me.


I am a Sixteen year old, avid coder, and investor. I currently attend Freehold Township High School and in my first year of the web design class. Some of my hobbies include track and cross country, lifting weights, coding, and playing video games. I am an entrepreneur at heart and someday wish to launch a business of my own.


I started the web design program in 2021, in the midst of a pandemic, but that did not slow me down. As the pandemic cooled down my skill level only increased as I learned HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. I continued to push on and I am now on the last few days of the web design program. I am happy to say that this year was very succsesful and I am looking forward to next year.
